Privacy & Legal Compliance FAQ

üîí FERPA & COPPA Compliance

How does the platform ensure FERPA compliance?

Our platform operates as a school official under FERPA 99.31(a)(1)(i). All data processing occurs within our SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We maintain detailed audit logs and provide data processing agreements that meet FERPA requirements.

What about COPPA age verification requirements?

The platform includes automated COPPA compliance checking and generates age-appropriate consent workflows. For students under 13, we provide parental consent templates and verification mechanisms that meet COPPA's verifiable parental consent requirements.

How long is our data retained?

Policy documents are retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days for transition purposes. Personal data is deleted within 30 days of subscription termination. Anonymized benchmarking data may be retained indefinitely but cannot be traced back to your district.

ü§ñ AI Training & Data Usage

Do you train AI models on our district data?

No. Your district's policies and documents are never used to train AI models. We use pre-trained models from OpenAI and Anthropic with strict data usage agreements. Your data is processed for analysis only and is not fed back into training pipelines.

Where is data processed and stored?

All data processing occurs within AWS regions in the continental United States. We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and follow NIST Cybersecurity Framework controls. No data is processed outside the US or shared with international entities.

Can we get a copy of our data?

Yes. You can export all your documents, reports, and analysis at any time in standard formats (PDF, DOCX, CSV). We provide full data portability and will assist with migration to other platforms if needed.
