Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI Blueprint™

How long does it take to complete the assessment and get a blueprint?

The AI readiness assessment takes about 5-10 minutes to complete. Once finished, you can immediately start the 4-step goal-setting wizard (3-5 minutes), and your personalized AI implementation blueprint will be generated in 10-30 seconds. Total time: 15 minutes from start to complete blueprint.

What makes the AI Blueprint different from generic strategic planning?

Our AI Blueprint is powered by GPT-4 and specifically trained on educational AI implementation best practices. It analyzes your NIST framework assessment scores, your specific goals, department needs, timeline, and budget to generate a truly personalized, phased implementation plan with specific tools, tasks, and recommendations tailored to your institution.

Can multiple team members access and collaborate on our blueprint?

Yes! Your subscription includes unlimited users per institution. You can share blueprints publicly via link or privately invite specific team members via email. All progress tracking updates in real-time so everyone stays aligned on implementation status.

Is our institutional data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade Supabase infrastructure with row-level security, encrypted data storage, and compliance with all major educational data protection regulations including FERPA. Your assessment data and blueprints are only accessible to your institution's authorized users.

What's included in the policy template library?

You get access to 50+ ready-to-use AI policy templates covering: AI governance frameworks, ethical AI use policies, faculty AI guidelines, student AI use policies, data privacy & security, acceptable use policies, AI literacy frameworks, and more. All templates are customizable and regularly updated.

Do you offer implementation support beyond the platform?

Yes! All subscriptions include email support and monthly expert webinars. We also offer consulting services for institutions that need hands-on support including strategy sessions, custom policy development, faculty training workshops, executive presentations, and implementation coaching. Contact us to learn more.

Can I try it before committing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all platform features including assessment, blueprint generation, progress tracking, policy templates, and resources. No credit card required. You can explore everything and decide if it's right for your institution.
