Privacy & Legal Compliance FAQ

🔒 FERPA & Institutional Compliance

How does the platform ensure FERPA compliance for all institution types?

Our platform operates as an institutional official under FERPA 99.31(a)(1)(i) for universities, community colleges, and trade schools. All data processing occurs within our SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure. We maintain detailed audit logs and provide institutional data processing agreements that meet FERPA requirements for all higher education types.

What about industry certifications and licensing compliance?

The platform includes automated analysis of industry certification and licensing requirements for trade schools and community colleges. We provide frameworks for program accreditation bodies and professional licensing organizations, including data consent and transparency requirements.

How long is institutional data retained?

Institutional documents are retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days. Student information follows your institution's retention policies regardless of institution type. Anonymized benchmarking data may be retained indefinitely but cannot be traced back to your institution.

🤖 AI Training & Data Usage

Do you train AI models on our institutional data?

No. Your institutional policies, research data, and student information are never used to train AI models. We use pre-trained models with strict institutional data agreements. Your data is processed for analysis only.

Where is sensitive data processed and stored?

All data processing occurs within FedRAMP-compliant AWS regions in the continental United States. We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and follow NIST Cybersecurity Framework controls. No data is processed internationally.

Can we export our institutional data?

Yes. Full data portability is provided in standard formats (PDF, DOCX, CSV). We assist with migration to other platforms and provide transition support during subscription changes.
