Grade-Level Appropriate Integration

Elementary (K-5)

Focus on interactive, visual, and gamified AI tools that support foundational learning.

  • Story creation and reading comprehension tools
  • Math fact practice with adaptive feedback
  • Simple coding and logic games
  • Art and creativity applications

Middle School (6-8)

Introduce more sophisticated tools that encourage critical thinking and collaboration.

  • Research and information literacy tools
  • Science simulation and modeling
  • Digital citizenship and AI ethics
  • Project-based learning platforms

High School (9-12)

Implement advanced AI tools that prepare students for future careers and higher education.

  • Advanced data analysis and visualization
  • AI model training and evaluation
  • Career-focused AI applications
  • Independent research projects
